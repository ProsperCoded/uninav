# UniNav Product Overview

UniNav is a collaborative university study materials platform that enables students to upload, share, and access essential course-related resources organized by faculty, department, and course level.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Centralized Resource Hub**: Replace scattered Google Drive links and group chats with a single, organized platform
- **Smart Discovery**: AI-powered recommendations based on user profile, academic interests, and peer exploration patterns
- **Community-Driven**: Students are both contributors and consumers, fostering collaborative learning
- **Monetization**: Contributors can earn from popular materials, blogs, and featured content

## Key Features

- **Organized Materials**: Categorized by Faculty → Department → Course with advanced search/filtering
- **Role-Based Access**: Students, Moderators, and Admins with appropriate permissions
- **Blog System**: Students write guides and articles, earn from engagement
- **Collections**: Curated groups of related materials
- **Review System**: Quality control through peer ratings and moderation
- **Notifications**: Real-time updates for approvals, uploads, and earnings
- **File Management**: Secure cloud storage with role-based access controls

## Target Users

- **Primary**: University students seeking organized access to study materials
- **Secondary**: Academic contributors looking to monetize their knowledge
- **Tertiary**: Educational institutions wanting structured resource sharing

## Business Model

- Freemium access to materials
- Paid promotions for boosted visibility
- Revenue sharing with content contributors
- Advertisement placements
